About Me

In July of 2016, I graduated from the University of Liverpool with first class honours. I specialize in heavy-load, backend technology and Artificial Intelligence. I especially enjoy the philosophical implications of AI, such as the concept of conciousness and sentience. Most of my interest in AI has stemmed from Asimov, who I consider a true genius and the forefather of modern science fiction.


I spend a large amount of my time enjoying videogames and often find myself working on various tools surrounding them (More information to come on these). For example, I'm currently hosting a Pokemon Tracking map on this domain for a few local friends found here. Keep in mind I'm simply running this locally (due to the costs of VPS hosting) through a a web DNS to hide my IP so there is no guarantee of it currently being up. I generally prefer goal orientated games such as MMORPGs as it allows me to do calculations to work out what the optimal playstyles are.


I'm also a big lover of techno and disco music and will often go to DJ sets. My current favourite artist is probably Dimtri From Paris.
